## Welcome to Balatro Mod Manager

Balatro Mod Manager is a standalone desktop application that makes modding Balatro simple and accessible. Browse hundreds of mods, install them with one click, and manage your entire mod collection from a beautiful, intuitive interface.

## Key Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="One-Click Installation" icon="mouse-pointer">
    Install mods with a single click. Dependencies are automatically resolved and installed.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mod Collections" icon="layer-group">
    Curated mod packs for different playstyles and difficulty levels.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Automatic Updates" icon="arrows-rotate">
    Keep your mods up to date with built-in update checking.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Backup & Restore" icon="clock-rotate-left">
    Save your mod configurations and restore them anytime.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Local Mod Import" icon="file-import">
